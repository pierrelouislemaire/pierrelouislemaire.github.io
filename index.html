Pierre-Louis Lemaire

Hi, I'm Pierre-Louis Lemaire, currently a research intern at Mila<\a> supervised by David Rolnick<\a> and Alex Hernandez-Garcia<\a>, working on geographical transferability of deep-learning base downscaling models. I recently graduate from a research MSc. in Applied Mathematics at Polytechnique Montréal supervised by Prof. Julie Carreau and Prof. Youssef Diouane.
Prior to coming in Montréal in September 2023, I was studying applied mathematics at INSA Toulouse in France.

My current research project involves using deep learning for precipitation downscaling to km-scale, focusing on enhancing geographical generalization skills of AI models. More broadly, I'm very interested on all works related to AI for science, with an emphasis on more fundamentals approaches for solving complex scientific challenges.
