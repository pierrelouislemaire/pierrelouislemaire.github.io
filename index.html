Pierre-Louis Lemaire

Hi, I'm Pierre-Louis Lemaire, a Research MSc. student in Applied Mathematics at Polytechnique Montréal supervised by Prof. Julie Carreau and Prof. Youssef Diouane.
Prior to settling in Montréal in September 2023, I was studying applied mathematics at INSA Toulouse in France.

My current research focuses on the application of physics-constrained deep learning to regional climate models emulation. More broadly, I'm interested in all applications of statistical learning methods that help to provide a better understanding of the Earth system.
